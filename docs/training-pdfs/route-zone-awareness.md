# Routes, Zones & Scheduling Awareness

## Zones & Counties

You will be assigned:

- **Specific zones** - Geographic areas you'll service
- **Specific counties** - County boundaries you'll work within
- **Specific customers** - Individual stops on your route

## Route Rules

- **Follow assigned order** - Routes are optimized for efficiency
- **Do not rearrange stops** without operator approval
- **Stay within assigned zone** - Do not service stops outside your zone

## If Problems Occur

**Contact your operator if:**

- Access is blocked
- Customer not available
- Bin is missing
- Safety issue occurs
- You need clarification on route

## Time Expectations

- Complete routes efficiently
- Do not rush or skip steps
- Maintain quality standards
- Follow the assigned route order

## Route Management

### Before Starting Your Route

- Review your assigned stops
- Check route order
- Verify you have all necessary equipment
- Contact operator if you have questions

### During Your Route

- Follow the assigned order
- Complete each stop fully before moving to the next
- Document any issues immediately
- Contact operator if problems arise

### After Completing Your Route

- Verify all stops are marked complete
- Submit all required photos
- Report any issues or concerns
- Confirm route completion with operator
