# Bin Blast Sticker Placement Guide

## Purpose

Stickers confirm service completion and brand consistency. Proper placement ensures visibility and professionalism.

## Placement Rules

- Place on **front-facing side** of the bin
- **Eye-level** when possible
- **Flat and visible** - ensure sticker adheres properly
- Do not cover existing labels or important information

## Proper Placement

The sticker should be:

- Centered on the front of the bin
- At a height that is easily visible
- Applied to a clean, dry surface
- Smooth with no bubbles or wrinkles

## Do Not

- Place on lid (may get damaged)
- Place on damaged surfaces
- Place crooked or peeling
- Cover customer's existing labels or markings

## Application Steps

1. Ensure bin surface is clean and dry
2. Remove sticker backing
3. Apply to front-facing side of bin
4. Smooth out any bubbles or wrinkles
5. Verify sticker is secure and visible

## Quality Check

Before leaving the stop:

- [ ] Sticker is properly placed
- [ ] Sticker is visible and readable
- [ ] Sticker is secure and won't fall off
- [ ] Sticker is not covering important information
