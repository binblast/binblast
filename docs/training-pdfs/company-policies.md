# Employee Accountability & Policies

## Payment Rules

You are paid **per completed cleaning**. Completion requires:

- Proper cleaning following SOP
- Required photos (inside and outside)
- Marking stop complete in system

## Training & Certification

- **Training required at hire** - All modules must be completed
- **Re-certification every 6 months** - Keep your certification current
- **Expired training = no routes** - You cannot work without valid certification

## Certification Requirements

To maintain certification:

- Complete all required training modules
- Pass all quizzes with 80% or higher
- Complete re-certification training every 6 months
- Stay current with policy updates

## Violations

Repeated issues may result in:

- Route removal
- Retraining requirements
- Termination

## Professional Conduct

As a Bin Blast employee, you are expected to:

- Show up on time for assigned routes
- Complete all stops with quality work
- Follow safety protocols at all times
- Submit required documentation (photos)
- Communicate issues promptly
- Respect customer property
- Maintain professional appearance

## Accountability

You are accountable for:

- The quality of your work
- Following all safety protocols
- Submitting required documentation
- Communicating issues or concerns
- Maintaining your certification

## Consequences of Non-Compliance

Failure to follow policies may result in:

1. **First offense** - Warning and retraining
2. **Repeated offenses** - Route removal or suspension
3. **Serious violations** - Immediate termination

## Questions or Concerns

If you have questions about policies or need clarification:

- Contact your operator immediately
- Review training materials
- Ask for help when needed
