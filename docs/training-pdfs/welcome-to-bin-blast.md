## Purpose

This guide introduces you to Bin Blast Co., your role, and what is expected of you as a team member.

## Who We Are

Bin Blast Co. provides professional trash can, dumpster, and sanitation services for residential and commercial customers. Our reputation depends on consistency, cleanliness, and professionalism.

## Your Role

As a Bin Blast technician, you are responsible for:

- Showing up on time
- Completing assigned cleanings correctly
- Following safety and photo documentation rules
- Communicating issues clearly

## Professional Standards

- Wear proper attire/PPE
- Be respectful of customer property
- Do not rush cleanings
- Never skip photo documentation

## Core Expectations

- **Quality over speed** - Take the time to do the job right
- **Safety first** - Always prioritize your safety and the safety of others
- **Photos are required for payment** - No exceptions
- **If unsure, contact your operator** - Communication is key to success

## Completion Reminder

You must complete training before being assigned routes.
