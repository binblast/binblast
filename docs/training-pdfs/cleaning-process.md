## Step-by-Step Cleaning

1. **Inspect bin before cleaning**
   - Check for damage
   - Note any issues
   - Document if necessary

2. **Remove loose debris**
   - Clear out any remaining trash
   - Dispose of properly

3. **Pressure wash inside thoroughly**
   - Ensure all surfaces are cleaned
   - Pay attention to corners and bottom

4. **Sanitize and deodorize**
   - Apply appropriate cleaning solutions
   - Ensure complete coverage

5. **Clean exterior surfaces**
   - Wash outside of bin
   - Remove any stains or marks

6. **Replace bin properly**
   - Return to original location
   - Ensure lid is secure

## Quality Standards

- No residue at bottom
- No visible grime on exterior
- Lid must be clean
- Area left neat

## Completion Checklist

Before marking a stop complete, verify:

- [ ] Bin fully cleaned
- [ ] Sticker intact and properly placed
- [ ] Photos taken (inside and outside)
- [ ] Stop marked complete in system
