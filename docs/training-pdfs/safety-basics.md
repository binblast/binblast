## Why Safety Matters

Trash cans and dumpsters may contain sharp objects, chemicals, insects, or hazardous waste. Your safety is our top priority.

## Required PPE

- **Gloves** (mandatory)
- **Eye protection** (recommended)
- **Closed-toe shoes** (mandatory)
- **High-visibility vest** (when applicable)

## Unsafe Situations

**Do NOT clean if you encounter:**

- Needles or medical waste
- Unknown chemicals
- Aggressive animals
- Severe spills requiring special equipment

**Report immediately to your operator.**

## Vehicle & Equipment Safety

- Secure all equipment
- Do not block traffic
- Never leave vehicle running unattended

## Slip & Fall Prevention

- Be aware of wet surfaces
- Use proper footing
- Avoid rushing

## Emergency Procedures

If you encounter a safety hazard:

1. **Stop immediately**
2. **Do not attempt to handle** dangerous materials
3. **Document the situation** with photos if safe to do so
4. **Contact your operator** immediately
5. **Flag the stop** in the system
